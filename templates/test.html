Term Tests (3)

There will be three term tests. They will all be one hour long each. Every term test is worth 15% of your overall final grade (i.e Test 1 is worth 15%, Test 2 is worth 15%, Test 3 is worth 15%) They will all be held in the Wednesday lecture time slot.

Final Exam

The final exam is worth 35% of your overall grade. More information regarding the final exam, such as the date and time, will be given closer to the end of the semester.
